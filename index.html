Who I Am

Hello, I'm Zach Tackett, a 2014 graduate from the University of Rio Grande in Rio Grande, OH where I obtained a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in Information & Network Security and a minor in Information Technology. I am currently the Assistant Webmaster for Ohio University's Alden Library. Previously I worked for Marshall University in the College of Education and Professional Development as Database Specialist for just under 2 years. Most of my jobs after college have been in Web Development, but I would eventually like to find a job that dwells more on the security or server admin side of things.
